Vegetable Stir Fry

One of the most classic recipes of mankind (at least to me) is the famous Vegtable Stir Fry. Pick your favorite vetables and just throw them on the pan and start cooking. You can add anything your heart desires. In just a few minutes, you have a healthy and, of course, a tasty dish waiting to be served!

Below are the steps

  1. Sauté: In a wok or large skillet add 1 Tablespoon olive oil over medium high heat. Add bell pepper, peas, carrots, mushrooms, broccoli, baby corn, and water chestnuts. Sauté 2-3 minutes until veggies are almost tender.
  2. Whisk: In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, garlic, brown sugar, sesame oil, chicken broth, and cornstarch.
  3. Mix: Pour over veggies and cook until the sauce has thickened.
  4. Garnish: add chopped green onions and sesame seeds if desired.
